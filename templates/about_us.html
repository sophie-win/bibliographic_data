About Hnin Hsu Han Thar Kyaw (Sophie)

Hnin Hsu graduated from University of Technology (Yatanarpon Cyber City), Myanmar, with a Bachelor of Engineering (Information Science and Technology) degree in 2016. After graduation, she started her career as an Android developer at a startup company in Yangon, developing point of sales app mainly targeted the retail shop in Myanmar for the two years. Hnin Hsu is proficient in application development, with strong technical skills in programming including Java, Android and database management. In 2018, she attended a business management diploma course from PSB, Singapore. There, she discovered her passion – using computers to solve business problems. To develop her skills further, she joined MSc (Information Systems) at the Nanyang Technological University in 2019.

Hnin Hsu is the youngest daughter in her family and the age gap between her and her elder sister is almost 12 years. She has five nieces and a nephew, and sometimes her cheery personality spreads to the people around her, especially when she talks about spending time with children. She also shares about her leisure-like activities with family and friends. Her hometown is surrounded by mountains and hills and so, she loves hiking. After she establishes her career, Hnin Hsu intends to travel around the world with her loved ones.

Hnin Hsu is responsible for web application development using the Django framework with Neo4j Graph database modeling. She also worked on the elegant User Interface design using jQuery and JavaScript capabilities. Moreover, she tried hard to support JSON format for nodes and hierarchical visualization with API generation to deal with the jQuery Ajax mechanism.
